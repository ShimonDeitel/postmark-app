## Description
Found an old stamp album in a drawer? Postmark tells you what's in it and what it's worth.

Photograph any postage stamp and get its country, issue, year, and a realistic sold-price range for both used and mint condition. Every find is mounted into your own digital album, organized by page.

WHAT IT DOES
- Identifies country, issue, year, denomination, and notable varieties
- Separate value ranges for used and mint condition, based on realistic sold prices
- One tap to real eBay sold listings for the exact stamp
- "Worth expertizing" flags for potential high-value varieties
- A real album: pages, notes, and a running collection total

FOR INHERITORS AND COLLECTORS ALIKE
Whether you inherited a shoebox of stamps or you're building a serious collection, Postmark answers the two questions that matter: what is it, and what's it worth.

POSTMARK PRO
3 identifications are free. Postmark Pro is $4.99/month or $29.99/year for unlimited identifications, auto-renews until cancelled, cancel anytime in Settings.

Estimates are informational only, not a formal appraisal. For insurance or sale of high-value stamps, consult a certified philatelic expertizing service.
